Sales

Please be aware that, due to the sophisticated nature of our cybersecurity solutions, S32 Technologies is subject to strict limitations on the clients to whom we can offer our services. Please read our sales policy below for additional information.

S32 Technologies Sales Policy

As a government contractor and U.S.-based cybersecurity services provider, S32 Technologies, herein referred to as "the Company", exclusively offers a majority of our services and solutions solely to governments, armed forces, law enforcement agencies, and critical infrastructure enterprises located in the United States (U.S.), United Kingdom (U.K.), Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. We emphasize that our services are strictly tailored to entities within these jurisdictions and sectors. 

Currently, only S32 Technologies  Domain Security,  Enterprise Hosting and SecureMail services are available for purchase without restriction. S32 Technologies continues to reserve the right to refuse, suspend, modify or cancel the services of any client, at anytime based on our sole discretion. All other products and services are offered only in line with this sales policy. 

The Company reserves the right to exercise sole discretion in determining whether a prospective client qualifies as a government, armed forces, law enforcement agency or critical infrastructure enterprise, as per our internal policies. 

If the prospective client is a critical infrastructure enterprise seeking our services, it is understood that the Company holds the exclusive authority to evaluate whether they meet the criteria for classification as a critical infrastructure enterprise, in accordance with our policy. This determination may be based on various factors, including but not limited to compliance with regulatory requirements, alignment with our business objectives, and risk assessment(s). Prospective clients are encouraged to engage in discussions with the Company to understand the criteria for classification as a critical infrastructure enterprise and to ascertain their eligibility for our services. By engaging with the Company, prospective clients acknowledge and agree to abide by these and other terms. 

We are unable to extend our services to government agencies, armed forces, law enforcement agencies, or critical infrastructure enterprises outside the jurisdiction of the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand, without explicit authorization from the U.S. Department of Treasury and U.S. Department of Defense. Exception: Domain Security, Enterprise Web Hosting & SecureMail.

The Company retains the authority to periodically reassess a client's eligibility in alignment with this policy. It is important to note that the Company holds exclusive discretion in determining a client's suitability for our services, in strict adherence to our sales policy and relevant regulations.

We appreciate your cooperation and understanding regarding these policies, which are designed to uphold legal and regulatory obligations. Please be advised that this sales policy is subject to change without prior notice. For further inquiries or clarification regarding our sales policy, please contact us directly.
